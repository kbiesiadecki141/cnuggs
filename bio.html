Meet The Team

Katelyn Biesiadecki

Katelyn, also known as “cakepin beaversmecki,” is a first year intended computer science major at University of California, Berkeley, who was involved in robotics in high school but now wallows in a pit of nothingness. Her passion for chicken nuggets has hindered her ability to get involved in college extracurriculars to the point where she spends every waking moment coding chickennuggs.com. She plays guitar but isn’t cool, and can also lick her elbow, though she is unwilling to share a live-action video to prove it. Like most depressed college students, she has an undying love for cartoons such as Futurama, Bob’s Burgers, and the Simpsons, though her IQ is unfortunately too low to understand Rick and Morty. Unlike most depressed college students, Katelyn has a shameful aversion to mashed potatoes and bacon that she hides behind a mask of pain and misery. Her favorite jams include your parents’ music, indie, and ???. Her favorite color is fried chicken. She has a blatantly masochistic preference for cats, as she is allergic to them but still prefers them to dogs. She also claims that her only flaw is the fact that she cannot tell the difference between Karen and Jenny -- a sad reality because Karen and Jenny don’t even know each other in real life.

Karen Lee

Karen, otherwise known as “Squaren Lee” or “yung yeet,” is a first year cognitive science major at University of California, Berkeley. Her infatuation with chicken nuggets ignited in high school when she faked a college admission letter to Hamburger University, transcended another plane of reality when she used a chicken nugget as a corsage for prom, and culminated in her co-ownership of chickennuggs.com. She is fond of dabbing and whipping and has curated her own dance move called the “whab,” which she claims will help her stake a claim during mating season. She enjoys creative writing, crying, exploring the world through spontaneous adventures, sobbing, trivia, wailing in distress, the work of Christopher Nolan, and occasionally whimpering. In the words of her roommate, her music is “utter trash.” She self-identifies as a BAG (basic Asian girl) versus an ABG, and has the tendency to pop Lactaid like ecstasy pills. She is also easily frightened. Unfortunately, she is the progenitor of multiple sons and must pay child support for, among other things, an inflatable Tyrannosaurus Rex and a six foot tall Chinese football player. One of Karen’s visions for the site was for it to be completely in Comic Sans, but Katelyn decided ardently against it. As a creative writer, Karen has explored several different genres and avenues of writing; among her best (yet underrated) works is the following poem:

Cheese Puffs
Cheese puffs are weird
Cheese puffs are fun
I like cheese puffs
But some just suck.

Jenny Chau

Jenny, who is definitely not Karen Lee and does not resemble Karen Lee in any shape or form, is a first year computer science major at University of California, Irvine. She describes herself as an egg -- either hard-boiled, soft-boiled, or raw, depending on her mood -- and is therefore the human embodiment of the Japanese character Gudetama. Her love of chicken nuggets, though undoubtedly true, pales in comparison to her decade-long committed relationship to boba tea. Like any dog lover, her lifelong dream is to fill a mansion with Shiba Inus and live there until she dies. Understandably, she doesn’t know what she’s doing with this site but still thinks it’s awesome.
